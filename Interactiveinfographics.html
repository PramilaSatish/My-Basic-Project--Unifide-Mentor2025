About Wine Types & Production

Red Wine

Made from dark-colored grapes. Popular types include Merlot, Cabernet Sauvignon, and Pinot Noir. Italy and France lead in red wine production.

White Wine

Produced using green grapes or red grapes with skin removed. Chardonnay and Sauvignon Blanc are global favorites.

Sparkling Wine

Includes Champagne and Prosecco. Produced through secondary fermentation to add bubbles, popular in France and Italy.

Wine Regions

Major wine-producing regions include Bordeaux (France), Tuscany (Italy), La Rioja (Spain), and Napa Valley (USA).
